## The Value of Distilled Human Intellect
Knowledgeable people and close friends act as living [[distilled-books]]. 

While reading physical or digital books provides broad, structured information, interacting with individuals provides dynamic, context-specific, and experiential wisdom. They filter raw data through their own life experiences, effectively pre-processing information and making it more accessible and relevant to my own growth.

## Core Strategy
- **Curate the "Library":** Treat relationships with intellectual peers as a primary knowledge source.
- **Active Engagement:** Engage in dialogue not just for social utility, but for information synthesis.
- **Feedback Loops:** Use these relationships to challenge existing models and test hypotheses.
