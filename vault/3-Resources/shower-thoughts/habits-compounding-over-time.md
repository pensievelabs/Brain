## Topic Overview
A reflection on how small, consistent habits can lead to significant cumulative effects over time. The idea that consistent, incremental actions, even seemingly insignificant ones, compound to create substantial long-term outcomes.

## Key Concepts
- The "1% better every day" principle.
- The power of consistency over intensity.
- Delayed gratification and long-term vision.
- The "compound interest" of personal development.
